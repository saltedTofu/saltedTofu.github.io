Shirley's Soup Kitchen

Recipes for the nostalgia of the past, yet hope for the future...

"Recipes to bring memories of family and children around the dinner table. Recipes for people who are lonely and yearning for comfort. Recipes for the nostalgia of the past, yet hope for the future..." Shirley's Soup Kitchen was founded on the five tenets of soupology, of which we can only reveal three to ensure the safety of our customers. One, every soup shall be served PIPING hot so as to burn the mouths of any casual soup enjoyer. Only soup royalty belongs here. Two, as we know, soup is a religious experience (ours especially). If you feel the need to get down on your knees and thank your god for blessing your taste buds on this day, feel free to do so. We will be offended if you don't. And three, every different type of spoon provides a different soup experience, and to honor this tenet we provide a variety of spoons to choose from. You're welcome.

Try Our Tomato

The tomatoes used in our tomato soup are harvested during the Texas tomato season and then are secretly transported to our underground cellar to be interrogated for their flavor secrets. We prefer a 'good cop, bad cop' routine to weasel out the tomatos that have no grit and determination to survive. Only the best tomatoes make it back upstairs to be squashed and turned into soup.
